Chairperson

  1. Home
  2. Chairperson

Dr. Asif Ali Khan (Tamgha – e – Imtiaz) is a visionary leader committed to advancing agricultural research and development in Pakistan. With decades of experience and numerous accomplishments, he continues to steer NSDRA towards innovation and excellence.

Message from the Chairperson

"It is an honor to lead the National Seed Development and Regulatory Authority. Our mission is to empower Pakistan’s agricultural sector by ensuring the availability of high-quality seeds and fostering innovation. Together, we can achieve self-sufficiency, improve crop productivity, and support the livelihoods of our farmers. Let us work towards a brighter and greener future for our nation."

- Prof. Dr. Asif Ali Khan

Qualifications

  • M.Sc. (Hons.) Plant Breeding & Genetics and MBA (MIS), Univ. of Agri, Faisalabad
  • Ph.D. Molecular Genetics and Stress Breeding, University of Liverpool, UK
  • Post Doc: Genomics, Univ. of California, Davis, USA

Professional Experience

  • Chairperson, National Seed Development and Regulatory Authority (2024 - Present)
  • UNESCO Chair on Low Carbon and Sustainable Agriculture (2023 - 2028)
  • Vice Chancellor, MNS University of Agriculture, Multan (2016 - 2024)

R&D Projects Completed

  • International Projects: 10 (USDA, USAID, DAAD, ACIAR, DIFID, NSFC)
  • National Projects: 8 (HEC, PARB, PSF, USDA Endowment Fund)
  • PhD Students Supervised: 17
  • Patents and Crop Varieties: 6 crop varieties and 17 patents
