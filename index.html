Welcome to NSDRA

The National Seeds Development and Regulatory Authority (NSDRA) is dedicated to transforming agriculture by developing a competitive and sustainable seed sector.

Our mission is to ensure food security, enhance agricultural productivity, and promote self-sufficiency in the seed industry. With cutting-edge technology and a commitment to quality, NSDRA plays a vital role in fostering innovation and supporting farmers nationwide.

Motto

"Transforming Seeds for a Greener Future"

Vision

To develop a competitive seed sector to ensure provision of technologically advanced planting material for enhancing agricultural productivity, food security, farm income, and export earnings.

Strategic Objectives

Formulation and implementation of National Seed Quality (NSQ) standards aligned with internationally accepted standards to meet domestic and export needs of the seed industry.

Mission

  • Ensure Food Security: Provide access to productive quality seeds.
  • Increase Crop Productivity: Facilitate introduction and development of germplasm and technologies, and R&D collaborations for quality seed production to improve crop yields.
  • Raise Farm Income: Promote development of new and improved high-yielding varieties and establish efficient seed systems.
  • Generate Employment Opportunities: Create new jobs across the seed sector, especially in R&D, seed production, processing, distribution, and export.
  • Self-Sufficiency and Import Substitution: Reduce reliance on seed imports by developing legislative frameworks that attract investment.
  • Export Promotion: Facilitate high-quality seed production for export markets.

CORRIGENDUM
Published on: September 16, 2025
Government of Pakistan — M/o National Food Security & Research
National Seed Development and Regulatory Authority (NSDRA)

In continuation of the National Seed Development and Regulatory Authority (NSDRA) Consolidated Advertisement No. 01/2025, published on 10-08-2025 in Daily Dunya and The News dated 11-08-2025, vide PID No. (I)1205/25, regarding recruitment against vacant posts in the NSDRA, it is notified that the last date for submission of Pay Order/Demand Draft in the name of NSDRA has been extended up to Friday 26-09-2025.

All applicants who have applied through the National Job Portal (NJP) and have not yet submitted their Pay Order are hereby advised to submit the required Pay Order/Demand Draft in the name of NSDRA in order to be considered further. All other terms and conditions of the advertisement shall remain unchanged.

The pay order/demand draft must be sent via registered courier at the following address:

National Seed Development and Regulatory Authority
Plot No. 25, Street No. 6, Sector H-9/1, Islamabad.
