CORRIGENDUM
Published on: September 16, 2025
Government of Pakistan — M/o National Food Security & Research
National Seed Development and Regulatory Authority (NSDRA)

In continuation of the National Seed Development and Regulatory Authority (NSDRA) Consolidated Advertisement No. 01/2025, published on 10-08-2025 in Daily Dunya and The News dated 11-08-2025, vide PID No. (I)1205/25, regarding recruitment against vacant posts in the NSDRA, it is notified that the last date for submission of Pay Order/Demand Draft in the name of NSDRA has been extended up to Friday 26-09-2025.

All applicants who have applied through the National Job Portal (NJP) and have not yet submitted their Pay Order are hereby advised to submit the required Pay Order/Demand Draft in the name of NSDRA in order to be considered further. All other terms and conditions of the advertisement shall remain unchanged.

The pay order/demand draft must be sent via registered courier at the following address:

National Seed Development and Regulatory Authority
Plot No. 25, Street No. 6, Sector H-9/1, Islamabad.

CORRIGENDUM
Published on: October 6, 2025
Government of Pakistan — M/o National Food Security & Research
National Seed Development and Regulatory Authority (NSDRA)

In continuation of the National Seed Development and Regulatory Authority (NSDRA) Consolidated Advertisement No. 01/2025, published on 10-08-2025 in Daily Dunya and The News dated 11-08-2025, vide PID No. (I)1205/25, regarding recruitment against vacant posts in the NSDRA, it is notified that the last date for submission of applications along with Pay Order/Demand Draft in the name of NSDRA has been extended up to Sunday, 12-10-2025 and are live at NJP portal.

  • Applicants who have already applied through the NJP and submitted their Pay Order are not required to re-apply.
  • Applicants who have applied but have not yet submitted the required Pay Order must do so in favor of NSDRA before the closing date to be considered further.
  • The Pay Order amount is Rs. 3,000/- for posts at Sr. No. 1 to 12, and Rs. 2,000/- for posts at Sr. No. 13 to 16.
  • Applications without pay order/demand draft will not be entertained. All other terms and conditions of the advertisement remain unchanged.
  • Applicants must clearly mention their Name with CNIC and the name of the post for which the Pay Order is being submitted, along with contact details.

The pay order/demand draft must be sent via registered courier at the following address:

National Seed Development and Regulatory Authority (NSDRA)
Plot No. 25, Street No. 6, Sector H-9/1, Islamabad.
Situation Vacant — NSDRA
Published on: August 10, 2025

The National Seed Development and Regulatory Authority (NSDRA), under the Ministry of National Food Security & Research, invites applications from suitable candidates for multiple merit-based positions.

  • Director (Admin/HR), Director (IT), Deputy Directors (IT, Horticulture, Legal, etc.)
  • Assistant Directors, Research Officers, Office Manager, Web Designer, APS, etc.
  • Support staff positions including Store Keeper, Driver, and Dispatch Rider.

Interested applicants must apply through the National Job Portal (NJP) and submit a Pay Order/Demand Draft in favor of “NSDRA” as per advertisement instructions.

Last Date to Apply: Within 15 days from publication.
